# Manual Test for 2x Speed Feature

## Steps
1. Open any video in the player.
2. Long press anywhere on the screen (except controls).
3. Verify that:
   - The video playback speed increases to 2x.
   - A "2x Speed" indicator appears on the screen.
   - Haptic feedback (vibration) is felt.
   - The screenshot menu DOES NOT appear.
   - The video DOES NOT pause.
4. Release the long press.
5. Verify that:
   - The video playback speed returns to normal (1x or previous setting).
   - The "2x Speed" indicator disappears.
